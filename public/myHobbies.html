Travel

My travelling started when I was nearly 12. My parents decided to take me and my siblings on a cross Canada road trip (instead of letting me play video games all summer break). Being so young, I did not appreciate the trip to the extent that I should have and this certainly was the same for next summer's, cross United States trip. I truly began to appreciate travel on my trip to Southeast Asia. Experiencing the difference in culture, food, etc.. is astounding. I followed this trip up with my most recent, a 5 month Europe trip and plan on going to the eastern coast of Asia for a few weeks in the near future.

Sports

I have played sports all my life and really enjoy playing team sports. I am also very interested in single person sports such as swimming and boxing. I played on several intramural teams during my time at University and on my school teams during high school.

Video Games/Board Games

I absolutely love playing video games and play a lot of Blizzard Entertainment games. Video games are an easy way to get lost in stories and become immersed. I have always enjoyed playing cooperative or single player games. Some of my favorites include Warcraft, Hearthstone and Stardew Valley. I am also very interested in Board Games and play during many board game nights with friends from school. Avalon was a huge hit for my group of friends and we play that quite often.

Languages

I have always been impressed with anyone that can speak languages other than their native languages and I believe it is important for people to learn languages other than their own. Communication in the world is essential and during my travels I realized how helpful being able to communicate with others is. I have learned a lot of German and can currently hold a conversation however, I definitely have a lot to learn. Following German I plan to try and learn Mandarin through a variety of programs including Rosetta Stone and Duolingo, much like I did for German.
